Welcome to The Surrey 1837 Club

The Surrey 1837 Club brings together Light Blue Freemasons from across the Province of Surrey through social events, lodge visits, learning opportunities, and shared experiences.

Our aim is simple:

  • To help Light Blues build friendships.
  • Feel connected to the wider Surrey masonic community.
  • Get more from Freemasonry in and out of their own lodge.
  • To support the learning and development of Light Blues.

Whether you are newly initiated, recently joined the Province, or simply looking to reconnect with other Surrey Light Blues, the 1837 Club is here to provide a welcoming and inclusive community.
